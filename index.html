What is Anime?

Who are not familiar with anime may think that it's just cartoon and technically it is , but it's more than just cartoon . While tradition cartoon targets children as their consumer , anime don't have such restriction and it targets audience of all age group. That's the most basic difference between cartoon and anime . But that's not all there is to anime , anime fans will also argue that anime should be limited to animation which is produced in Japan and by Japan. So generally, western cartoons are not considered "anime" . Anime and Japan are inseparable to the point that , many outsiders develop their opinions on Japan through anime. Anime is one of key factors in establishing Japan's soft power all over the world .

Anime in India

Anime in India is not as popular in other parts of world . One of the main reason behind is that anime is not availability of anime in regional local languages and there is also not any legal streaming services which focus on presenting anime content in India. Crunchyroll is available in India but only some are available for Indian audience which compare to what other parts of world get to have , is like a fraction of it . Still anime's popularity is growing in India in recent times , particularly among youths.
